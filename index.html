Welcome to Skyrain

A dream of non-profit humanitarian crypto token system

Our Mission

Skyrain is an experimental, non-profit crypto token designed to resist malicious manipulation and channel transparent, community-reviewed aid to real people in need.

Our goal is to build a system where trust, fairness, and relief are embedded into the code itself — not dependent on middlemen.

How It Works

Fixed Supply

A maximum of 10 Billion Skyrain tokens will ever exist. No inflation.

Community Pool

62% of supply dedicated to community acquisition in approved swap avenue.

Relief Pool

30% of supply dedicated to community-reviewed airdrops for humanitarian aid.

Operation Pool

7% of supply dedicated to the project operation and outreach. 1% of supply dedicated to emergency conditions.

Buffer Pool

A Buffer Pool grows with every swap, providing rebalance strength against manipulation.

DAO

Community governance reviews relief requests and operation fee and ensures transparent distribution.
