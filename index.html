Welcome to Skyrain

A non-profit humanitarian cryptocurrency system

Our Mission

Skyrain is an experimental, non-profit cryptocurrency designed to resist market manipulation and channel transparent, community-reviewed aid to real people in need.

Our goal is to build a system where trust, fairness, and relief are embedded into the code itself — not dependent on middlemen.

How It Works

Reserve

A reserve pool grows with every trade, providing buyback strength against manipulation.

Relief

30% of supply dedicated to community-reviewed airdrops for humanitarian aid.

DAO

Community governance reviews relief requests and ensures transparent distribution.
